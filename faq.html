Frequently Asked Questions

Who are your teachers?
All our teachers are British-trained and qualified, with experience teaching students from all over the world.
What’s the class size?
Classes are kept small—usually 8-12 students—to ensure plenty of speaking practice and personal support.
Can I try a class first?
Yes, we offer a free trial lesson or assessment for most courses. Contact us to book yours!
How do I register?
Simply fill out our contact form and we’ll guide you through the process.
Are your courses suitable for beginners?
Absolutely! We have levels for complete beginners up to advanced learners.
Where are you located?
Our centre is in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. We also offer online classes worldwide.
