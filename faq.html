Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses, fees, registration, and more.

What courses does Britannia offer?

We offer General English, IELTS Preparation, Business English, and Young Learner programmes. Check our Courses page for details.

How do I register for a course?

Simply contact us or fill out our online enquiry form. Our team will guide you through every step.

What are the course fees?

Fees depend on the course type and duration. Contact us for the latest fee schedule and promotions.

Do you offer online or in-person classes?

Yes, we provide both options to fit your needs and schedule.

Can I join if I am a complete beginner?

Absolutely! Our courses are designed for all levels, from beginner to advanced.

What is the class schedule?

We offer flexible schedules including weekdays, weekends, and evening classes. Reach out for the current timetable.

Will I get a certificate after completing a course?

Yes, you will receive an official certificate from Britannia Language Centre upon successful completion.
